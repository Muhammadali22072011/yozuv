# Yozuv landing ‚Äî copy & CRO rework (Uzbek, Latin)

Guiding principle from the audit: every claim maps to a real feature. Headline shifts from *what the product does* to *the outcome the owner gets* (fewer no-shows, more bookings, less manual work).

*CRO rationale:* current H1 states a mechanism, not a benefit; the new headline ties to the ICP's exact pain (notebook chaos, no-shows) and the subhead front-loads the reminder benefit + removes the two biggest objections (no website, no app) within the 5-second scan.

# Conversion improvements beyond copy

1. **Add real social proof above the fold (highest impact).** The page has zero trust numbers. Add a strip under the hero CTA: `Toshkent, Samarqand va Buxorodagi 100+ biznes Yozuv'dan foydalanmoqda` plus 2‚Äì3 short owner testimonials with name + business type + city (e.g. *"Neyavkalar 3 barobar kamaydi" ‚Äî Sardor, barber, Toshkent*). Even a logo/avatar row of business types beats nothing. Use real numbers only once available ‚Äî don't fabricate.

2. **Cut signup friction ‚Äî show the Telegram entry, not `/auth/login`.** The ICP won't "log in"; they live in Telegram. Make the primary CTA open the Telegram Mini App / bot directly (deeplink) with a one-tap "Telegram orqali kirish" rather than routing to a generic login page. This removes the single biggest drop-off for a non-technical, app-averse audience.

3. **Add a short FAQ / objection-handling section before the final CTA.** The ICP's real hesitations: *"Sayt kerakmi?" (yo'q), "Ilova o'rnatish kerakmi?" (yo'q), "To'lovni qanday qilaman?" (Payme/Click, sumda), "Sinovdan keyin avtomatik yechib olinadimi?" (yo'q ‚Äî karta kerak emas), "Sozlash qiyinmi?" (5 daqiqa, biz yordam beramiz)*. Four Q&As kill the top conversion blockers.

4. **Add a final repeat-CTA band + sticky mobile CTA.** Mobile is the ICP's primary device, but the only CTAs are in the hero and pricing. Add a closing section (`Bugun yozilishlarni qabul qilishni boshlang` + button) and a sticky bottom bar on mobile (`Bepul boshlash`) so the action is always one tap away during scroll.

5. **Inject light urgency / activation nudge without fake scarcity.** Avoid fake countdowns (erodes trust with this tight-knit community). Instead use honest momentum framing near the CTA: `Sozlash 5 daqiqa ‚Äî bugun birinchi yozuvni qabul qiling` and a referral hook (`Do'stingizni taklif qiling ‚Äî ikkalangizga ham qo'shimcha oy bepul`), which fits the doc's referral channel and the high trust in professional micro-communities.
